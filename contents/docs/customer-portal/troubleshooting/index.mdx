## Portal Access Issues

### "Team Not Found" Error

If you or your customers see a **"Customer Portal - Team Not Found"** error when trying to access the portal, the teamID in the URL is either missing or invalid.

{/* TODO: Screenshot - the "Customer Portal - Team Not Found" 404 error page */}

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Check the URL format">
    The Customer Portal URL must include a `teamID` query parameter that identifies your organization. Make sure the URL follows this format:

    ```
    https://your-domain.com/portal?teamID=your_company_name
    ```

    <Note type="warning">
    The `teamID` parameter is **case-sensitive** and must match exactly how it was configured in the system.
    </Note>
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Verify the teamID value">
    Confirm you are using the correct teamID for your organization. If you are unsure of the correct value, contact your system administrator.

    Common mistakes:
    - Extra spaces or special characters in the teamID
    - Using an old or renamed teamID
    - Copying the URL without the `?teamID=...` portion
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Clear browser cookies">
    The portal stores your team information in a browser cookie. If you previously accessed the portal with a different or invalid teamID, stale cookies may cause issues.

    Clear your browser cookies for the portal site and try again. In most cases, the system automatically clears invalid cookies, but a manual clear can help if problems persist.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Contact support">
    If you continue to see the error after verifying the URL and clearing cookies, there may be a configuration issue on the backend. Contact your administrator or Weevio support for assistance.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

### Portal Page Not Loading

If the portal URL is correct but the page does not load or shows a generic error:

- **Check your internet connection** and try refreshing the page.
- **Try a different browser** or open an incognito/private window to rule out extension or cache issues.
- **Wait and retry** — if the system is temporarily unable to connect to the database, you will see a "unable to retrieve team information" message. This is usually resolved within a few minutes.

<Note type="info">
If your customers report portal access problems, share the direct portal link with the correct `teamID` parameter included. This is the most common fix for access issues.
</Note>
