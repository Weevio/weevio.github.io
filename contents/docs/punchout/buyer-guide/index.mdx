This guide explains how PunchOut buyers experience custom pricing when browsing products through SAP Ariba or other procurement systems.

## What to Expect

When you access a supplier's Shopify store through your SAP Ariba PunchOut catalog, you'll automatically see **your negotiated pricing** without needing to log in or create an account.

### How It Works for You

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Browse Your Catalog">
    In SAP Ariba (or your procurement system), search and browse products from your supplier's catalog.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Click a Product">
    When you click a product link, it opens the supplier's store in your browser with your custom pricing automatically applied.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="See Your Prices">
    All product prices shown are **your negotiated prices**, not public prices. This persists as you browse different products.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Add to Cart & Purchase">
    Add items to cart and complete your purchase. Your pricing remains consistent throughout the entire process.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

<Note type="success">
**No Login Required**: You don't need to create a Shopify account or remember any passwords. Your identity is automatically recognized when you click through from your procurement system.
</Note>

## Recognizing Your Custom Pricing

### How to Tell You're Seeing Custom Pricing

When your custom pricing is active:

- **Prices match your contract**: Products show the negotiated prices you expect
- **Consistent across products**: All products display your customer-specific pricing
- **Same as cart transfer**: Prices match what you'll see when you transfer your cart back to SAP Ariba

### Visual Indicators

Most stores will display pricing normally, but you may notice:

- Product prices that differ from the store's public pricing (if you've seen them before)
- Special price labels or badges indicating customer pricing (store-dependent)
- Your company name or customer ID may appear in the header (store-dependent)

<Note type="info">
The exact visual appearance depends on how your supplier has configured their store. The important thing is that the **prices you see are accurate** for your customer account.
</Note>

## Browsing Experience

### Viewing Products

**Your custom pricing applies to**:
- Individual product pages
- Collection/category pages (if prices are shown)
- Search results
- Related products and recommendations

**Navigation**:
- Use the store's navigation menu to browse categories
- Use search to find specific products
- Click product links from collections or search results
- Your pricing persists across all pages

### Session Duration

Your custom pricing session:
- **Starts**: When you click through from SAP Ariba
- **Duration**: Lasts up to **12 hours**
- **Ends**: When you close the browser tab OR after 12 hours

<Note type="warning">
**After 12 hours**, you'll need to click through from SAP Ariba again to refresh your custom pricing. If you leave the tab open longer than 12 hours, you may see public pricing instead.
</Note>

### Multiple Browser Tabs

If you open multiple products in different browser tabs:

**Each tab maintains its own session**:
- Tab 1: Your pricing (clicked from SAP Ariba)
- Tab 2: Your pricing (opened from Tab 1)
- Tab 3: Public pricing (if opened directly without PunchOut)

**Best Practice**: Open new tabs by clicking links within the store to maintain your pricing session.

## Shopping & Checkout

### Adding Items to Cart

When you add products to your cart:

1. **Cart shows custom pricing**: Your negotiated prices are maintained
2. **Totals are accurate**: Subtotals and totals reflect your prices
3. **No surprises**: What you see is what you'll pay

### Standard PunchOut Cart Transfer

After adding items to your cart, you can:

**Option 1: Transfer Cart to SAP Ariba** (Traditional PunchOut)
1. Click the "Transfer Cart" or "Punch Back" button
2. Your cart is sent back to SAP Ariba with all items
3. Complete the purchase order in SAP Ariba
4. Your pricing remains consistent

**Option 2: Direct Checkout** (If enabled)
1. Proceed to Shopify checkout
2. Complete payment through the store
3. Pricing matches what you saw while browsing

<Note type="info">
Most B2B buyers will use **Option 1 (Cart Transfer)** to maintain procurement workflow approvals. Check with your procurement team for your organization's policy.
</Note>

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: First-Time PunchOut User

**Situation**: You're new to using PunchOut with this supplier.

**What to do**:
1. Log into your SAP Ariba account (or procurement system)
2. Navigate to your supplier's catalog
3. Browse and click on any product
4. The supplier's store opens in a new window/tab
5. Verify you see reasonable pricing (not $0 or blank)
6. If pricing looks wrong, contact your supplier's support

### Scenario 2: Pricing Looks Wrong

**Situation**: You see prices that don't match your contract.

**Possible reasons**:
- You didn't click through from SAP Ariba (opened store directly)
- Your session expired (> 12 hours)
- Your customer account isn't tagged correctly in Shopify
- Technical issue with the integration

**What to do**:
1. Close the browser tab
2. Go back to SAP Ariba
3. Click the product link again
4. If pricing still looks wrong, contact supplier support with:
   - Your SAP Ariba buyer ID
   - Screenshot of the pricing issue
   - Date and time you accessed the store

### Scenario 3: Multiple Buyers on Same Computer

**Situation**: Multiple people in your organization need to browse from the same computer.

**What to do**:
1. **Each buyer uses a separate browser tab**
2. Each person clicks through from their own SAP Ariba session
3. Close your tab when done (to prevent others from seeing your pricing)

**Important**: Closing the tab ensures your pricing doesn't carry over to the next person.

### Scenario 4: Session Expired

**Situation**: You left a tab open overnight and now see different prices.

**What to do**:
1. Close the expired tab
2. Return to SAP Ariba
3. Click through again to start a fresh session
4. Your custom pricing will be restored

## Privacy & Security

### What Information Is Stored?

When you browse with PunchOut:

**Stored locally (in your browser)**:
- Your SAP Ariba buyer ID (e.g., "AN01631249352")
- Timestamp of when you clicked through

**NOT stored**:
- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No credit card details
- No passwords

### How Long Is Data Kept?

- **Browser session**: Up to 12 hours
- **After closing tab**: Immediately cleared
- **On supplier servers**: Resolution cached for 15 minutes

### Multiple Buyers on Shared Computer

**Privacy protection**:
- Each browser tab has isolated storage
- Closing your tab clears your buyer ID
- Other users can't see your pricing in new tabs

**Best practice**: Always close your browser tab when finished to prevent others from accessing your pricing session.

## Troubleshooting for Buyers

### I See "$0.00" or Blank Pricing

**Possible causes**:
- Supplier hasn't configured pricing for you yet
- Product is out of stock
- Technical issue with widget

**Solution**:
- Contact supplier support
- Provide your SAP Ariba buyer ID
- Mention specific products with pricing issues

### Prices Changed While Browsing

**Possible causes**:
- Your session expired (> 12 hours)
- Browser tab was refreshed without fromIdentity parameter
- You navigated to a page outside the PunchOut session

**Solution**:
- Close tab and click through from SAP Ariba again
- Verify URL includes your buyer ID parameter
- Contact support if problem persists

### "Access Denied" or Error Messages

**Possible causes**:
- Your customer account isn't set up in Shopify
- Store configuration issue
- Your buyer ID isn't recognized

**Solution**:
- Contact supplier support immediately
- Provide your SAP Ariba buyer ID
- Include screenshot of error message

### Pricing Doesn't Match Purchase Order

**Possible causes**:
- Pricing changed between browsing and cart transfer
- Product has time-sensitive pricing
- Different pricing rules in SAP Ariba vs. Shopify

**Solution**:
- Review your purchase order in SAP Ariba before approving
- Contact supplier if there's a significant discrepancy
- Pricing in SAP Ariba is always the final price

### Cart Transfer Fails

**Note**: This is a separate feature from fromIdentity pricing.

**Solution**:
- See supplier's PunchOut cart transfer documentation
- Contact supplier support for cart transfer issues
- Provide order details and error messages

## Tips for Best Experience

### Before You Start
- Ensure you're logged into SAP Ariba or your procurement system
- Use a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Allow pop-ups from SAP Ariba (for opening store links)

### While Browsing
- Always click through from SAP Ariba for first product
- Open new products by clicking links within the store
- Don't bookmark product pages (bookmarks won't include your buyer ID)
- Check pricing on first product to confirm it's working

### When Finished
- Close the browser tab when you're done shopping
- Don't leave tabs open overnight (session expires)
- Transfer cart to SAP Ariba before closing (if you haven't checked out)

### For Teams
- Each team member should click through from their own SAP Ariba account
- Don't share browser tabs between buyers
- Close tabs to prevent pricing confusion for other users

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to create a Shopify account?

**No.** Your SAP Ariba buyer ID automatically identifies you and shows your custom pricing. You don't need to create an account or log in.

### Can I bookmark products to view later?

**Not recommended.** Bookmarks won't include your buyer ID parameter, so bookmarked pages will show public pricing instead of your custom pricing. Always access products through SAP Ariba for accurate pricing.

### How long does my pricing session last?

**12 hours** from when you first click through from SAP Ariba. After 12 hours, you'll need to click through again to refresh your session.

### Can I browse on my phone?

**Yes**, if your procurement system supports mobile access. Click through from SAP Ariba on your mobile device just like you would on desktop. The same 12-hour session rules apply.

### Will my pricing work across all suppliers?

**It depends.** Each supplier must implement the fromIdentity pricing feature. Contact your suppliers to ask if they support PunchOut custom pricing during browsing.

### What if I see different prices in SAP Ariba vs. the store?

This shouldn't happen if the integration is working correctly. The prices you see while browsing should match the prices in your cart transfer. If there's a discrepancy:
1. Take screenshots of both prices
2. Note the product SKU or name
3. Contact supplier support

### Can I use discount codes or coupons?

**Check with your supplier.** Some suppliers may allow additional discounts on top of your custom pricing, while others may restrict coupon usage for B2B customers.

### Is my browsing data tracked?

**No.** The feature only stores your buyer ID locally in your browser to display correct pricing. No browsing history or personal data is tracked or stored beyond standard e-commerce analytics.
