When a drop-ship order is fulfilled by Ingram Micro or TD Synnex, Weevio Cloud can automatically send the customer a branded shipping notification email containing their tracking numbers and order details. This keeps customers informed without any manual effort.

## How It Works

When a vendor ships a drop-ship order, Weevio Cloud:

1. Detects the shipment event from Ingram Micro (via webhook) or TD Synnex (via polling)
2. Verifies that all notification conditions are met (see [When a Notification Is Sent](#when-a-notification-is-sent))
3. Sends the customer a branded email with clickable tracking numbers and a summary of the shipped items

## Prerequisites

Before enabling shipping notifications, make sure the following are in place:

| Requirement | Details |
|:------------|:--------|
| **Shopify Vendor Integrations add-on** | Your account must have the Shopify Vendor Integrations feature purchased |
| **Vendor integration connected** | At least one vendor (Ingram Micro and/or TD Synnex) must be connected and active |
| **SMTP configured** | Your SMTP email settings must be configured in Weevio Cloud so that notification emails are sent from your own domain |

<Note type="warning">
If SMTP is not configured, shipping notification emails cannot be sent. Contact Weevio support if you need help setting up SMTP.
</Note>

## Enabling Shipping Notifications

The feature is controlled by a toggle in Weevio Cloud under **Integrations > Shopify > Vendor Settings**.

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open Vendor Settings">
    In Weevio Cloud, navigate to **Integrations > Shopify** and scroll down to the **Vendor Settings** section.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Enable the Toggle">
    Under the **Shipping Notifications** heading, turn on the **Enable automatic shipping notification emails** toggle. Once enabled, additional configuration options appear.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Configure Options (Optional)">
    Two optional fields are available:

    - **Test Email Override** — Enter an email address to redirect all notifications to that address instead of customers. Useful for testing before going live. See [Testing Before Going Live](#testing-before-going-live).
    - **BCC Tracking Notifications** — Enter one or more comma-separated email addresses that should receive a blind copy of every shipping notification. Useful for internal shipment tracking or compliance.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Done">
    Your changes are saved automatically. Customers will begin receiving tracking emails for new shipments.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

<Note type="note">
The Shipping Notifications setting applies to **all** vendor integrations (Ingram Micro and TD Synnex) simultaneously. You do not need to enable it separately for each vendor.
</Note>

## Testing Before Going Live

Weevio Cloud provides two ways to test shipping notifications before customers receive them:

### Test Email Override

When a **Test Email Override** address is set in Vendor Settings, all shipping notification emails are redirected to that address instead of the customer. The email subject line is prefixed with `[TEST]` so you can easily identify test emails in your inbox.

<Note type="note">
While a test email override is active, no customers will receive shipping notifications. Remove the override address when you are ready to go live.
</Note>

### Send Test Email

Click the **Send Test Email** button in the Shipping Notifications section of Vendor Settings. This sends a sample email with placeholder data (a fictional customer name, order number, and two sample tracking numbers) so you can preview exactly how the email will look with your branding.

If you have BCC addresses configured, they will also receive the test email.

## When a Notification Is Sent

A tracking email is sent only when **all** of the following conditions are met:

| Condition | Details |
|:----------|:--------|
| **Shipping Notifications enabled** | The toggle must be turned on in Vendor Settings |
| **Drop-ship order** | The order must be flagged as a drop-ship-to-customer order in PIMS |
| **Customer email allowed** | The customer record must not have the "Do Not Email" flag set |
| **Email address on file** | A ship-to or bill-to email address must exist on the order |
| **New tracking numbers** | The vendor must have provided at least one new tracking number |

If any condition is not met, no email is sent and the order continues to be fulfilled normally without notification.

## What the Email Contains

Each shipping notification email is branded with your organization's identity and includes the following:

| Section | Details |
|:--------|:--------|
| **Logo** | Your organization's logo (if configured in Weevio Cloud) |
| **Header** | A branded header bar using your primary theme color with the text "Your Order Has Shipped" |
| **Greeting** | Personalized with the customer's ship-to name |
| **Tracking disclaimer** | A note below the greeting reminding customers that it may take up to 24 hours for the carrier to update tracking information |
| **Shipment table** | A table with columns for **Item** (product description), **Qty** (quantity shipped), **Serial #** (shown only when serial numbers are available), and **Tracking Number** (clickable link to track the shipment) |
| **Order identifiers** | Displays applicable identifiers: **Order #** (Shopify reference number), **Invoice #** (PIMS order number), and **Customer PO #** (purchase order number) |
| **Footer** | A closing message with your organization name |

<Note type="note">
Pricing is intentionally **not** included in shipping notification emails to protect sensitive pricing information.
</Note>

## Supported Vendors

### Ingram Micro

Ingram Micro shipment events are received via webhook. When Ingram ships a drop-ship order, the webhook fires immediately and Weevio Cloud sends the customer notification without delay.

### TD Synnex

TD Synnex tracking numbers are retrieved by a background polling process that checks for new shipments once per hour. Once new tracking numbers are detected and written to PIMS, the customer notification is sent automatically.

## Customer Experience

Customers receive a single email per shipment containing everything they need to track their order. Each tracking number in the email is a clickable link that opens a tracking search in a new tab. Customers do not need to log in to any portal.

When serial numbers are provided by the distributor, a **Serial #** column appears in the shipment table so customers can identify exactly which unit they received. The email also includes a brief disclaimer noting that tracking information may take up to 24 hours to appear with the carrier.
