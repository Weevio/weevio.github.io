The Weevio Shopify Integration connects your PIMS system and Shopify store into a unified commerce platform. Start by connecting your store, then explore the two major feature areas:

- **Store Connection** — Connect one or more Shopify stores to Weevio Cloud using OAuth (recommended) or legacy API credentials.
- **Widget** — A product page enhancement that provides real-time inventory visibility, shipping estimates, smart recommendations, B2B pricing, and more directly on your Shopify storefront.
- **PIMS Sync** — A continuous background process that automatically synchronizes products, inventory levels, and orders between PIMS and Shopify.
