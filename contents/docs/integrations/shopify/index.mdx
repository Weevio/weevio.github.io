The Weevio Shopify Widget is a powerful product page enhancement that seamlessly integrates into your Shopify store to provide customers with real-time inventory information, accurate shipping estimates, and intelligent product recommendations. Designed to reduce cart abandonment and increase conversions, the widget delivers a premium shopping experience for both B2C and B2B customers.

[Screenshot: Product page showing the Weevio widget with shipping estimates and availability information]

## What It Does

The Weevio Shopify Widget enhances your product pages with:

- **Real-time inventory visibility** across Shopify and multiple distributor sources
- **Accurate shipping estimates** based on customer location with local pickup options
- **Smart product recommendations** when items are out of stock
- **Cross-sell suggestions** to increase average order value
- **B2B wholesale pricing** with PunchOut integration support
- **Enhanced variant selection** with improved user experience

## Key Features

The widget offers seven powerful features that can be enabled individually or combined based on your business needs:

### 1. Improved Variant Selection

Replace Shopify's default variant selector with an enhanced, card-based interface that displays price differences, prevents invalid combinations, and offers full keyboard navigation.

[Screenshot: Card-based variant selector showing color and size options]

### 2. Shipping Estimates & Availability

Show customers accurate delivery estimates and real-time stock availability. Choose between Basic (static messaging) or Premium (zip code-based, USPS integration) plans.

[Screenshot: Shipping estimates showing "Standard delivery in 3-5 business days" and local pickup availability]

### 3. In-Stock Recommendations

Automatically suggest alternative products when the selected item is out of stock or has low inventory, helping customers find what they need without leaving your store.

[Screenshot: Modal showing in-stock alternatives with price comparisons]

### 4. Cross-Sell Recommendations

Show complementary products after customers add items to their cart, increasing average order value with intelligent product associations.

[Screenshot: Cross-sell modal displaying related products after add-to-cart]

### 5. B2B Wholesale Pricing

Display negotiated pricing for logged-in business customers automatically, showing "Your Price" badges with discounts and customer-specific rates.

[Screenshot: Product page showing B2B pricing with "Your Price" badge and discount amount]

### 6. Multi-Source Inventory

Aggregate stock from multiple sources (Shopify, Ingram Micro, TD SYNNEX) to show accurate availability and reduce out-of-stock situations.

### 7. PunchOut Commerce

Seamless integration with SAP Ariba, Coupa, and other procurement systems, allowing business buyers to shop without logging in while seeing their negotiated rates.
