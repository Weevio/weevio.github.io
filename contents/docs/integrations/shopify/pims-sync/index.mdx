Weevio Cloud runs a continuous background process that keeps PIMS and Shopify in sync. Once configured, products, inventory levels, and orders flow automatically between the two systems.

## Sync Directions

At a high level, there are two sync directions:

- **Inventory Sync (PIMS to Shopify)**: Products and quantity levels flow from PIMS to Shopify. When you update an inventory item in PIMS, the changes automatically propagate to your Shopify store.
- **Order Sync (Shopify to PIMS)**: When a customer places an order on Shopify, the order is automatically created in PIMS via webhook, including all line items, shipping charges, and discounts.

## Prerequisites

Before setting up PIMS Sync, ensure the following are in place:

- **PIMS Platinum or Gold** with a configured server URL
- **At least one Shopify store connected** in Weevio Cloud (Settings → Integrations → Shopify)
- **PIMS shipping and discount part codes** created for order sync
