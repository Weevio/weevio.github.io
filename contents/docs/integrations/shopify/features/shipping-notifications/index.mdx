When a drop-ship order is fulfilled by Ingram Micro or TD Synnex, Weevio Cloud can automatically send the customer a shipping notification email containing their tracking number and order details. This keeps customers informed without any manual effort.

[Screenshot: Vendor Shipping Notifications toggle in Integrations > Shopify > Vendor Settings]

## What It Does

When a vendor ships a drop-ship order, Weevio Cloud:

- Detects the shipment event from Ingram Micro (via webhook) or TD Synnex (via polling)
- Checks that all notification conditions are met
- Sends the customer an email with their tracking number and a summary of the shipped items

## Enabling Shipping Notifications

The feature is controlled by a toggle in Weevio Cloud under **Integrations > Shopify > Vendor Settings**.

<Step>
  <StepItem title="Open Vendor Settings">
    In Weevio Cloud, navigate to **Integrations > Shopify** and scroll to the **Vendor Settings** section for Ingram Micro or TD Synnex.
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Enable the Toggle">
    Turn on the **Shipping Notifications** toggle.

    [Screenshot: Shipping Notifications toggle in the Vendor Settings panel]
  </StepItem>
  <StepItem title="Save">
    Your changes are applied immediately. Customers will begin receiving tracking emails for new shipments from that vendor.
  </StepItem>
</Step>

<Note type="note">
The Shipping Notifications setting is configured independently for each vendor. You can enable it for Ingram Micro, TD Synnex, or both.
</Note>

## When a Notification Is Sent

A tracking email is sent only when **all** of the following conditions are met:

| Condition | Details |
|:----------|:--------|
| **Shipping Notifications enabled** | The toggle must be turned on in Vendor Settings |
| **Drop-ship order** | The order must be flagged as a drop-ship-to-customer order |
| **Customer email allowed** | The customer must not have opted out of email communications |
| **Email address on file** | A ship-to or bill-to email address must exist on the order |

If any condition is not met, no email is sent and the order is fulfilled normally.

## What the Email Contains

The notification email includes:

- **Tracking number(s)** for the shipment
- **Item descriptions and quantities** for each shipped line item

<Note type="note">
Pricing is intentionally **not** included in shipping notification emails to protect sensitive pricing information.
</Note>

## Supported Vendors

### Ingram Micro

Ingram Micro shipment events are received via webhook. When Ingram ships a drop-ship order, the webhook triggers immediately and Weevio Cloud sends the customer notification without delay.

### TD Synnex

TD Synnex tracking is retrieved by a polling process that checks for new shipments at regular intervals. Once a shipment is detected, the customer notification is sent automatically.

## Customer Experience

Customers receive a single email per shipment containing everything they need to track their order. They do not need to log in to any portal — the tracking number and carrier information are included directly in the email.
